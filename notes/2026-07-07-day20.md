## Exp060 — Persistent Swahili TAR Cache Prep

### Objective
Eliminate repeated `.tar.xz` indexing for Swahili spontaneous audio.

### Hypothesis
The Swahili slowdown is caused by repeatedly opening compressed TAR archives and rebuilding member indexes for individual samples. Caching open TAR handles and member indexes per shard should reduce repeated archive scans.

### Scope
Exp060 will only change Swahili audio loading behavior.

### Success Criteria
- Each Swahili TAR archive is indexed once per run.
- Member lookup is reused for subsequent samples from the same archive.
- Swahili validation/training throughput improves compared with Exp059.
- No model, LoRA, learning-rate, or dataset-size changes.

### Next Step
Patch `src/datasets/afrivoices_dataset.py` to cache TAR handles and member indexes by archive path.
