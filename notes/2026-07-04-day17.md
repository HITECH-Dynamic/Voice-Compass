## Swahili Infrastructure Pivot

Today confirmed that the six-language manifests and ANV indexing infrastructure are working correctly.

The remaining bottleneck is the Swahili audio resolver.

Testing showed that the resolver repeatedly:
- lists repository contents,
- searches all matching TAR archives,
- scans archive members,
- extracts a single file,

for every requested audio sample.

Although functionally correct, this approach required many hours to complete decode filtering and is not suitable for rapid experimentation.

After reviewing the architecture, the decision was made to stop optimizing around repeated TAR searches and instead implement a permanent Swahili TAR index mirroring the existing ANV Parquet index.

This will become reusable infrastructure for all future multilingual experiments.
