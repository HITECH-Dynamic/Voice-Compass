Observations:

Increasing the dataset size from 1000 to 2000 examples improved performance. WER continued to decrease until approximately step 900 before slightly worsening at step 1000.

Conclusion:

Additional data produced larger gains than simply increasing training duration. More data appears to be the most promising direction.
